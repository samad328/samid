Hello! I'm Abdul Samad, a passionate and creative front-end web developer with a strong background in computer science. I have been coding and building for the web since 2018, specializing in front-end development with a keen focus on performance, usability, and aesthetics.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and began my professional journey at a local software house. There, I honed my skills, working on a range of projects and technologies as a front-end web developer. My experience has equipped me with a solid understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks.

Currently, I am a freelancer based in the beautiful city of Abu-Dhabi, UAE. I enjoy the freedom and flexibility of freelancing, which allows me to work on diverse projects and continuously learn and adapt to new challenges. My goal is to create visually appealing, high-performing websites that provide an excellent user experience without compromising on creativity.

Feel free to reach out if you're looking for a dedicated and skilled front-end developer to bring your web projects to life!
